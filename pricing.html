Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free tier work?
When you sign up, you automatically get a free forum with up to 1,500 users. No credit card required. You can upgrade anytime as your community grows.
What happens when I reach my user limit?
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. Existing users remain active, but new signups will be paused until you upgrade.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Cancel anytime and your forum will automatically downgrade to the free tier at the end of your billing period. Your data is never deleted.
Is PGP messaging really secure?
Yes! Messages are encrypted client-side using 4096-bit RSA keys. The server only sees encrypted ciphertext. Only the recipient with the private key can decrypt messages.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
