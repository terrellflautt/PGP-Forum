Our Philosophy

SnapIT Forums is built for whistleblowers, journalists, and privacy advocates. We use zero-knowledge architecture to ensure we literally cannot read your private messages.

What We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Google Account Email: For authentication via OAuth
  • Username: Your chosen @username for anonymous inbox
  • Public Forum Posts: Unencrypted posts in public Shards
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio (optional)

Automatically Collected

  • Metadata: Timestamps, sender/recipient IDs
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system

What We CANNOT Collect

Due to our zero-knowledge architecture, we mathematically cannot access:

  • Private Message Content: Encrypted with recipient's PGP public key, we hold no decryption key
  • Encryption Keys: Generated in browser with .extractable=false, impossible to export
  • Your IP Address: When using anonymous relay (routed through 3-5 random nodes)
  • Deleted Messages: 7-pass DoD secure deletion makes recovery impossible

How We Use Your Information

Authentication

  • Verify your identity via Google OAuth
  • Create and maintain your account

Service Delivery

  • Route encrypted messages to intended recipients
  • Display your public posts in forums
  • Calculate your Power (upvote score)
  • Enable shard creation for eligible users

Security & Moderation

  • Prevent abuse, spam, and illegal activity
  • Enforce Terms of Service
  • Respond to legal requests (we can only provide non-encrypted data)

Data Storage

Encrypted Data

Private messages are stored as encrypted ciphertext in AWS DynamoDB. We cannot decrypt them, even if legally compelled to do so.

Auto-Deletion

Messages with auto-delete enabled are:

  • Deleted automatically at scheduled time
  • Overwritten with 7 passes of random data (DoD 5220.22-M)
  • Forensically unrecoverable
  • Removed from all backups

Backups

We do NOT perform traditional backups. All data is ephemeral by design. If our database is destroyed, all data is permanently lost.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except:

Service Providers

  • AWS: Hosting infrastructure (DynamoDB, Lambda, CloudFront)
  • Google: OAuth authentication
  • Stripe: Payment processing (for premium features)

Legal Obligations

If legally required, we may disclose:

  • Your email address
  • Your username
  • Public forum posts
  • Metadata (timestamps, user IDs)

We CANNOT disclose:

  • Private message content (encrypted, we have no key)
  • Deleted messages (forensically unrecoverable)
  • Encryption keys (browser-only, non-extractable)

Your Rights

Access & Portability

You can export all your data at any time from your account settings.

Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. This will:

  • Permanently delete your profile and account data
  • Remove your username from the platform
  • Delete all your public posts
  • NOT delete: Messages already sent to others (they control their inbox)

Opt-Out of Tracking

We use minimal analytics. You can:

  • Use browser "Do Not Track" settings

  • Use anonymous relay for IP protection

Cookies

No third-party tracking cookies.

Security Measures

Encryption

  • End-to-End: 4096-bit RSA PGP encryption for private messages
  • Transport: TLS 1.3 for all connections
  • At-Rest: AWS encryption for database storage

Anonymous Relay

  • Messages routed through 3-5 random WebRTC relay nodes
  • Server only sees relay IP, not your real IP
  • Onion-routing style anonymity

Non-Extractable Keys

  • Private keys generated with .extractable=false flag
  • Browser API enforces hardware-level protection
  • Keys stored in IndexedDB, inaccessible to JavaScript

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Disclose what data was compromised
  • Provide recommended actions

Note: Encrypted messages cannot be compromised without the private key, which we don't have.
